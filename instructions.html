INSTRUCTIONS

STORYLINE

In this game you will have to complete a race in which you will be given multiple choices. Depending on what you choose you will end up with a different ending. The game has multiple elements that will allow you to gain more points, gain more time and even an extra life. These are explained more in detail further down. As soon as you start the game, the timer will start. You will have 5 minutes to complete the game before time runs out and you die. While playing you will be able to mute/unmute sounds and pause/resume the game

HOW DO I MOVE?

With your keyboard! As simple as that. Use the arrows on your keyboard to move around. UP, DOWN, LEFT to RIGHT...

EXTRA TIME?

The game will be timed but you will be given the opportunity to gain more time by collecting mushrooms while playing. These will be hidden around the screen so that you find them difficult to find. Some will be easier than others

LIFE COUNT

Once you start the game you only have 1 life. If you make a bad decision you will be dead without having the possibility to resume playing. However, you will be able to gain an extra life by collecting stars that will be hidden around the screen just like the mushrooms

PROGRESS BAR

At all times a progress bar will be displayed below the game title. This will show you how far you have come and how much you have left to finish the race/game. Whenever you press a button, depending on what type of choice you make, the progress bar will or will not progress
